About Us

Our company is based on the belief that our customers' needs are of the utmost importance. Our entire team is committed to meeting those needs. As a result, a high percentage of our business is from repeat customers and referrals. We would welcome the opportunity to earn your trust and deliver you the best service in the industry.

Location: We are mobile and are by appointment only. We will come to you. Now serving Columbus and Vicinity. Reserve some time for us,and we will reserve some time for you.

Mission Statement

Next to Godliness Mobility Cleaning Services, LLC is dedicated to helping you achieve your highest potential by helping you emit positive impulses of victory and newfound confidence. We will strive to strengthen and fortify your desire for continued independence in today's health conscious society. We are a mobility cleaning company that will literally stand behind what is close to you…your mobility equipment! Next to Godliness Mobility Cleaning Services exists today because we recognize the importance of being in an overall cleaner and safer environment. We will always value this long awaited service and your continued right to possess it. We are here to serve you.

Our History and Purpose

Next to Godliness Mobility Cleaning Services was founded and established in the summer of 2009 by Todd Stokes in Columbus, Ohio.
